About Us

Revo Hospital was established as a small nursing home in Nairobi in 2014 and growing over the years to become one of the biggest players in the secondary healthcare market with a network of 5 fully fledged Hospitals and 12 primary care Medical Centres.
We are dedicated to providing quality medical care at an affordable cost. We have well-equipped facilities in Nairobi, Kajiado, Thika, Nakuru, Eldoret and Kisumu. Our services include inpatient and outpatient services.
